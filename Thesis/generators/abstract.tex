\begin{abstract} 

\noindent 
In this work, we do cryptanalysis on the Hawk digital signature scheme using the \textit{Learning a Parallelepiped} method which broke the GGH and basic NTRU digital signature schemes.
This work shows that this method does not pose a threat to Hawk, as it is not possible / requires too many signature samples.
\end{abstract}
